Global Citizen Festival 2023: Red Hot Chile Peppers, Ms. Lauryn Hill, Jung Kook, Anitta, Stray Kids, and More Live from Central Park

This year's Global Citizen Festival is headlined by Red Hot Chili Peppers, Ms. Lauryn Hill, Jung Kook, and Anitta, with performances from Conan Gray, D-Nice, Sofia Carson, and Stray Kids. The Global Citizen Festival campaign is driving urgent action to address the major systemic issues our world is facing, including the climate crisis, equity for women and girls and the global hunger crisis.
