Description : The Music from the Basement TV program, which has already become a cult, was created by producer Nigel Godrich in 2006 and shows live performances of musicians without a host and without an audience. The transfer was conceived with the aim of documenting the creative process. In total, for the period 2006-2009, 2 seasons of 6 episodes each were filmed. Each episode included performances by several musicians. Due to problems with sponsors, the cycle did not continue for a long time. Only in 2011 did Radiohead's performance recorded in 2008 and in 2012 by The Shins come out as a separate release .
